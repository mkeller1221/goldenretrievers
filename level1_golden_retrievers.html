Why golden retrievers have been America's favorite breed for decades subtitle

Golden retrievers are one of the most popular dog breeds in the United States. They are known for being friendly, loyal, and easy to trainbold. Originally bred in Scotland in the 1800s to retrieve birds for hunters, they now thrive as family pets, therapy dogs, and guide dogs.

A golden retriever puppy at eight weeks old. caption

Golden retrievers make great pets for many reasons. Here are some of their best qualities:

  • They are gentle with young children and babies
  • They love to swim and play fetch outdoors
  • They learn new commands faster than most breeds
  • They rarely show aggression toward people or other animals

Sidebar: fun facts sidebar
The golden retriever's famous "soft mouth" means they can carry a raw egg without breaking it! This trait was bred into them so they could return hunted birds undamaged. Today, it makes them excellent service dogs — they can carry objects without dropping or crushing them.
